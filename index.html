The Maker

Meet Selena

Selena's always had an eye for color — the kind of person whose biology notes looked like works of art, every concept mapped out in perfectly chosen gel-marker shades. That same instinct for pairing colors and finding beauty in patterns followed her through a career in science research, until she found the perfect medium for it.

For years, she's been crocheting blankets, pillows, shawls, hats, scarves, tops, Christmas stockings — every piece is a chance to play with color and texture and make something that feels like it was made just for you, because it was. The custom work is what she loves most: hearing someone's vision, picking the perfect palette, and creating something completely one-of-a-kind.

What started as a creative outlet quickly turned into a waitlist. So she made it official. Stitch Reflections is the result of hundreds of hours of stitching, an obsession with premium materials, and the joy of making something beautiful by hand.

— Selena

How It Works

The Custom Order Process

Every piece begins with a conversation. Here's what to expect when you commission a custom creation.

01

Share Your Vision

Tell me about the piece you're imagining — colors, size, who it's for, and what feeling you want it to carry.

02

Design & Quote

I'll put together a design concept with material options and a detailed quote for your approval.

03

Creation

Once confirmed with a deposit, I begin crafting your piece — with progress updates along the way.

04

Delivery

Your finished piece is carefully packaged and delivered to your door, ready to be loved.
