The Maker

Meet Selena

What started as a creative outlet during an uncertain time has grown into something deeply personal. With a background in neuroscience and psychology, Selena brings an intuitive eye for color, texture, and the emotional resonance of handmade objects.

Every blanket, every pillow, every stitch carries intention — designed not just to decorate a space, but to transform how it feels. Using premium, plush materials and a meticulous attention to detail, Stitch Reflections creates heirloom-quality pieces that are meant to be treasured.

— Selena

How It Works

The Custom Order Process

Every piece begins with a conversation. Here's what to expect when you commission a custom creation.

01

Share Your Vision

Tell me about the piece you're imagining — colors, size, who it's for, and what feeling you want it to carry.

02

Design & Quote

I'll put together a design concept with material options and a detailed quote for your approval.

03

Creation

Once confirmed with a deposit, I begin crafting your piece — with progress updates along the way.

04

Delivery

Your finished piece is carefully packaged and delivered to your door, ready to be loved.
